The Complete Sherlock Holmes

Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's Sherlock Holmes series is a collection of four novels and 56 short stories featuring the brilliant detective Sherlock Holmes and his loyal friend Dr. John Watson. The novels, including "A Study in Scarlet," "The Sign of the Four," "The Hound of the Baskervilles," and "The Valley of Fear," showcase Holmes' deductive prowess as he unravels complex mysteries. The short stories, compiled in collections such as "The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes," "The Memoirs of Sherlock Holmes," "The Return of Sherlock Holmes," and "His Last Bow," span a range of intriguing cases. Set in Victorian and Edwardian London, these works highlight Holmes' keen observation skills, logical reasoning, and penchant for disguises, establishing him as one of the most iconic and enduring fictional detectives in literary history.
The Complete Sherlock Holmes consists of all of the 56 short stories and four novels written by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.
